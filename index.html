Current work: Master's thesis @ MPI-SWS

I am implementing a software-based cache partitioning mechanism, called page coloring, on Linux PREEMPT-RT. In order to reduce interference caused by shared caches on multicore platforms, a portion of the last-level cache is exclusively dedicated to real-time applications, improving their predictability. In this implementation, kernel processes are reserved an exclusive (different) partition in the last-level cache, as well. This effectively leads to a higher degree of isolation compared to previous implementation efforts.
